Our Yoga Asanas

Tadasana

Mountain Pose - A foundational standing pose that promotes strength, stability, and proper posture. Perfect for beginners to establish body awareness.

Vrikshasana

Tree Pose - Improves balance, focus, and mental clarity. This standing pose strengthens the legs and opens the hips.

Bhujangasana

Cobra Pose - Opens the heart chakra and strengthens the spine. Great for building core strength and improving flexibility.

Adho Mukha Svanasana

Downward Dog - Energizes the body and stretches hamstrings, calves, and shoulders. One of the most fundamental poses in yoga.

Balasana

Child's Pose - A calming and restorative pose that relieves tension. Perfect for relaxation and meditation.

Trikonasana

Triangle Pose - Enhances flexibility, relieves back pain, and strengthens legs and core. A fundamental standing pose.
